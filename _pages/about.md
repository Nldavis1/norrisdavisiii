Norris Davis III is a PhD candidate in the Department of Political Science at UC Berkeley. His dissertation investigates how Black media ecosystems shape political attitudes and facilitate political socialization within Black communities. Drawing on survey experimental methods and causal inference, his work contributes to scholarship on political communication, media effects, and race and politics.

He also holds research interests in AI governance and election security, and has prior professional experience in policy advocacy, AI model evaluation, and trust and safety.cpages/academicpages.github.io/discussions).
